# ANZC Brisbane November 2025 – VRS Tier 2 Open LAN

We’re excited to announce that **ANZC LAN** will return for its final LAN event of the 2025 calendar year — hosted at **DFRAG Studios** in **Brisbane, QLD, Australia** — and will officially feature a **Valve Regional Standings (VRS)** attachment.

In accordance with **Valve’s Tier 2 VRS ruleset**, this event will operate as an **Open Registration, first-come, first-served LAN**.

---

## Event Details
- **Dates:** Saturday 29 – Sunday 30 November 2025  
- **Location:** DFRAG Studios, Brisbane QLD Australia  
- **Region:** Oceania (sub-region of Asia for VRS purposes)  
- **Tier:** 2 (Open LAN Qualifier – No Direct Invites)  
- **Visa Region:** Australia  
- **VRS Filter:** Oceania (sub-region of Asia VRS list)  
  - Rosters must include a majority of players residing in Oceania (Australia, New Zealand, or Pacific territories)

---

## Registration
Registration will be conducted via a **Google Form** that automatically records and publishes submission timestamps to a **public, view-only Google Sheet** for transparency in accordance with **VRS § 4.4**.

- **Opens:** November 7  
- **Closes:** November 14  
- **Qualified Teams Announced:** November 15  
- **Format:** Open / First-in, Best-Dressed (VRS Compliant)  
- **Process:** Registration link will be posted 7 days before opening as required under VRS rules  
- **Entry Fee:** $250 AUD per team ($50 per player)  
- **Substitutes & Coaches:** Permitted as per VRS guidelines  

---

## Prize Pool – $4,000 AUD
- 1st Place – $2,500  
- 2nd Place – $1,000  
- 3rd Place – $500  

---

## Format
All matches will be played **on LAN** at **DFRAG Studios, Brisbane, QLD**.

### Group Stage
- Two GSL (double-elimination) groups of 4 teams  
- Opening matches – Best of 1  
- All subsequent matches – Best of 3  
- Top two teams from each group advance to Playoffs  

### Playoffs
- Double-elimination bracket  
- Opening matches – Best of 1  
- All following matches – Best of 3
