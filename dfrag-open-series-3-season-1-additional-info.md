# DFRAG Open Series 3 – Additional Information

## Tournament Dates
- **Stage 1 (Open Qualifier):** January 24–25, 2026  
- **Stage 2:** January 31–February 1, 2026  
- **Stage 3:** February 7–8, 2026  

---

## Tournament Format
- **Stage 1:** Open Qualifier, double-elimination, open to all eligible rosters  
- **Stage 2:** Double-elimination group stage (2 groups of 4)  
- **Stage 3:** 4-team double-elimination playoff  

- **Tournament stages:** Online  
- **Platform:** FACEIT  

---

## VRS Information
- **Tournament Tier:** Tier 2  
- **Ranked Stages:** Stage 2 and Stage 3  
- **Direct VRS Invites:** None (Open Qualifiers only)  
- **VRS publication date used for ranking:** January 2026  

---

## Open Qualifier
- Open to eligible rosters from **Australia and New Zealand**  
- Registration via FACEIT  
- No roster-specific or discretionary selection criteria
