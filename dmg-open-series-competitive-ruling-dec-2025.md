## Competitive Ruling Notice

Following a review by Tournament Operations, a competitive ruling has been issued against **Ground Zero Gaming**.

### Summary
**Ground Zero Gaming** forfeited scheduled matches in a DFRAG-operated VRS event while participating in another tournament, without providing sufficient notice to Tournament Operations.

### Decision
Effective immediately, **Ground Zero Gaming** is disqualified from all VRS-eligible events hosted by DFRAG for a period of **two (2) months**.

This ruling applies only to DFRAG-operated events.

- Start: 16/12/2025 (dd/mm/yyyy)
- End: 16/02/2026 (dd/mm/yyyy)

### Rationale
Teams may participate in multiple tournaments; however, timely communication of scheduling conflicts is mandatory. Failure to do so impacts competitive integrity, opposing teams, and event operations.

### Closing
DFRAG remains committed to fair, professional, and transparent competition.

No further public comment will be made.
