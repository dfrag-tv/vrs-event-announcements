## Eligibility

### Minimum Age
All participants must be at least **15 years of age** at the start of the event.  
*Note: venue minor policy may apply due to liquor licensing.*

### Region
All participants must reside in:
- Australia  
- New Zealand  

### Existing Ban
Any participant with an **active ban** from FACEIT, ESL, or Valve is prohibited from participating.  
Tournament Organiser reserves the right to restrict players at their discretion.

---

## Team

### Restrictions
#### In-Game Names (Alias)
Aliases may not:
- Contain vulgar or offensive language  
- Contain sponsor or product names  
- Duplicate another participant’s alias  

Organiser may restrict any alias at any time.

#### Multiple Teams
A participant cannot compete on more than one team.

#### Team Size
Each team consists of:
- 5 Players  
- Up to 2 Substitutes  
- 1 Coach  

All must meet **Eligibility** requirements.

### Team Continuity
Teams must maintain **at least 3 Core Members** throughout the event.  
- Core Members = the original five registered players.  

### Substitutes
- Must be listed 24h prior to match start.  
- Can only be used before round 1 of a map.  
- Coaches cannot serve as substitutes.  

### Emergency Substitutes
Allowed only for verified emergencies (medical, power outage, etc.) at organiser discretion.

### Coaches
- May not be in the server during live rounds.  
- May communicate only during tactical pauses or between maps.  

### Extrajudicial Agreements
“Gentlemen’s agreements” are not recognised or enforceable.

### Team Transfer
Transfers after the start require organiser approval.

### Roster Lock
Organiser will communicate roster lock dates before event start.

## Tournament Structure
All matches played **on LAN at DFRAG Studios, Brisbane QLD**.  

### Group Stage
- Two GSL (double-elimination) groups of 4 teams  
- Opening matches – Bo1  
- All following matches – Bo3  
- Top two teams from each group advance to Playoffs  

### Playoffs
- Double-elimination bracket  
- Opening matches – Bo1  
- All following matches – Bo3

## Game Specific Rules

### Game Version
- Latest **Counter-Strike 2** version at event time.  
- If unstable, organiser may use a previous patch.

### Map Pool
- de_ancient  
- de_overpass  
- de_dust2  
- de_inferno  
- de_mirage  
- de_nuke  
- de_train  

### Veto Procedures
#### Bo1
1. A bans 1  
2. B bans 1  
3. A bans 1  
4. B bans 1  
5. A bans 1  
6. B bans 1  
- Remaining map played. Knife round for starting side.  

#### Bo3
1. A bans 1  
2. B bans 1  
3. A picks 1 (B chooses start side)  
4. B picks 1 (A chooses start side)  
5. A bans 1  
6. B bans 1  
- Remaining map played. Knife for side.  

#### Bo5
1. A bans 1  
2. B bans 1  
3. A picks 1 (B chooses start side)  
4. B picks 1 (A chooses start side)  
5. A picks 1 (B chooses start side)  
6. B picks 1 (A chooses start side)  
- Remaining map played. Knife for side.  

### Map Advantage
Upper-bracket winner may start with a 1-0 map advantage if stated in advance.

### Server / Match Settings
```
mp_roundtime 1.92
mp_freezetime 20
mp_startmoney 800
mp_maxrounds 24
mp_overtime_maxrounds 6
mp_overtime_startmoney 12500
```

### Communication
- **LAN:** coaches may speak only during tactical pauses and map breaks.  
- **In-game chat:** for admin communication only.  

### Tactical Timeouts
- 3 × 30 sec per regulation match  
- 1 × 30 sec per overtime  

### Technical Pauses
- Max 5 mins per team per match unless organiser approves longer.

### Admin Pause
Admin may pause or restart rounds at any time if required.

## Anti-Cheat
- **No FACEIT Anti-Cheat is used for this LAN.**  
- Servers are hosted and monitored locally by DFRAG staff.  
- **Match-fixing & Collusion:** zero tolerance.  
  - Any manipulation of results = disqualification + ban from future DFRAG events.  
- **Betting Restrictions:** players may not wager on matches they participate in.  
- **Equipment Policy:**  
  - PCs, monitors, peripherals, and network infrastructure provided by DFRAG.  
  - Players may bring personal peripherals (mouse, mousepad, keyboard, headset).

## Cheating

### Config Files
Allowed unless they grant an advantage.

### Scripts
Macros and scripts prohibited.

### Drivers
Modified drivers prohibited.

### Overlays
All third-party overlays disabled.

### Game Files
No file modification permitted.

### Bugs / Glitches
Intentional exploitation forbidden.

### Positions
No pixel-walks or unintended exploits.

### Other Software
Strictly banned:
- Aimhacks  
- Wallhacks  
- Radarhacks  
- Any external assistance software

## Fair Play & Active Participation
- Teams must field **five active players** at all times.  
- AFK, idling, or deliberate inactivity is not permitted.  
- Violations may lead to round, map, or event forfeits.
