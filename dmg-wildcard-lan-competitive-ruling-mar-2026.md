# Competitive Ruling Notice

## DFRAG Wildcard LAN - Tournament Ruling

On **March 14, 2026**, during the **DFRAG S1 Wildcard LAN**, an incident occurred following **map one of the Lower Bracket Final** between **Mindfreak** and **Ground Zero**.

The incident involved **Matthew "Texta" O'Rourke** of Mindfreak and a player from Ground Zero.

The matter was initially reported by a **DFRAG Media Group staff member**.

## Investigation

Tournament Operations conducted a full investigation which included:

- Direct communication with both involved parties
- Review of **security camera footage** from the venue
- Review of **event media recordings**

Following the investigation, Tournament Operations determined that disciplinary action was required.

## Decision

Effective immediately, **Matthew "Texta" O'Rourke** is banned from **all DFRAG-operated and DFRAG-partnered tournaments** for a period of **six (6) months**.

- **Start Date:** 14/03/2026
- **End Date:** 14/09/2026

## Emergency Substitute Approval

Due to these exceptional circumstances, **Mindfreak** has been granted permission to use **coach Tyler "tucks" Reilly** as an **emergency substitute player** for the remainder of the following events:

- **DFRAG Wildcard LAN S1**
- **ANZC Locals Brisbane 2026**

This exemption applies only for the duration of these events.

## Conduct Policy

DFRAG maintains a **zero tolerance policy** regarding **physical aggression or violence** at its events.

All participants are expected to uphold professional conduct and maintain the integrity of the competition environment.

## Closing

DFRAG remains committed to maintaining a safe, fair, and professional competitive environment for all participants.

No further public comment will be made regarding this matter.
